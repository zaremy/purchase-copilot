# Target Segments

A multi-dimensional analysis identifying 30+ distinct segments, prioritized into 4 strategic tiers for phased rollout.

---

## Tier 1: MVP Targets (Months 1-6)

**Focus:** High-Anxiety Consumers

Mass market consumers with high pain points and willingness to pay for confidence.

### 1A. The Anxious Family
**Buying a minivan/SUV for safety**

- **Profile:** Novice-Casual buyer, highly risk-averse, brings a partner.
- **Pain Point:** "What if I buy a death trap for my kids?"
- **Market Size:** 2.5M buyers/year

### 1B. Budget Commuter
**First car or daily driver**

- **Profile:** Student/Young Pro, limited budget, solo buyer.
- **Pain Point:** "I can't afford a $2,000 repair bill."
- **Market Size:** 2M buyers/year

### 1C. Work Truck Buyer
**Tradesperson needing reliability**

- **Profile:** Contractor, needs truck for income, pragmatic.
- **Pain Point:** "If this truck breaks down, I don't get paid."
- **Market Size:** 1.5M buyers/year

---

## Tier 2: Early Expansion (Months 6-12)

**Focus:** Enthusiasts & Advisors

Higher knowledge users who influence others and buy more frequently.

### 2A. The Enthusiast
**Buying specific performance cars**

- **Need:** Deep, model-specific technical details (e.g., bore scoring).

### 2B. The Advisor
**"Car Guy" helping friends**

- **Need:** Sharing features and "proof" to show the buyer.

### 2C. Luxury Buyer
**High-end private party**

- **Need:** Verification of features and maintenance history.

---

## Tier 3: Pro & B2B (Year 2-3)

**Focus:** Professionals & B2B

High-volume users needing workflow efficiency and reporting.

### 3A. Independent Mechanic
**Mobile PPI Services**

- **Opportunity:** Pro Tier ($29.99/mo) for client reporting and payments.
- **Market:** 50K mechanics performing 3M PPIs/year.

### 3B. Used Car Dealer
**Wholesale Inventory Buyer**

- **Opportunity:** Team accounts ($199/mo) for auction inspection speed.
- **Market:** 40K independent dealers.

---
