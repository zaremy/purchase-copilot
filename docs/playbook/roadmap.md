# Product Roadmap

A phased approach to validate the core consumer value proposition before expanding into professional and specialty markets.

---

## Phase 1: MVP Launch (Months 1-6)

**Status: Complete**

Focus on the "Nervous Navigator" (Tier 1). Validate that the Contextual Checklist + Risk Score reduces anxiety and drives conversion.

### Core Features
- Manual Checklist & Photo Capture
- Basic VIN Decoding (Year/Make/Model)
- Guided Inspection Workflow (Offline)
- Risk Scoring & PDF Export

### Success Metrics
- 1,000 Paid Users
- 4.5+ App Store Rating
- 30% Checklist Completion Rate
