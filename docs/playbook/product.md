# Product Overview

**PRODUCT STRATEGY**

A comprehensive suite of tools designed to replace the "gut feeling" of buying a used car with data-driven confidence.

---

## The User Journey

1. **Capture** - Scan VIN & decode specs automatically
2. **Contextualize** - LLM generates custom checklist
3. **Inspect** - Guided walk-around & photos
4. **Analyze** - Risk scoring & comparison
5. **Negotiate** - Data-backed offer generation

---

## Core Features

### Contextual Checklist
Dynamically generated inspection items based on the specific Make, Model, and Year.

- Base 68-point universal inspection
- 3-5 vehicle-specific "Killer Issues" (e.g., BMW Vanos)
- Visual guides for what to look for

### Risk Scoring Engine
Objective 0-100 score to quantify vehicle condition and risk.

- Weighted scoring (Engine > Cosmetic)
- "Deal Breaker" flags for major issues
- Comparison against market averages

### Comparison Matrix
Side-by-side evaluation of multiple vehicles to support decision making.

- Compare Price vs. Condition Score
- Highlight pros/cons of each option
- "Best Value" recommendation

### Negotiation Builder
Scripts and talking points generated from inspection findings.

- "Ask for $X off because of Y issue"
- Polite but firm scripts for nervous buyers
- Exportable summary for the seller

---

## MVP Scope Boundaries

### IN SCOPE (MVP)
- Manual VIN entry & decoding
- Contextual checklist generation (LLM)
- Photo capture & storage
- Basic Risk Score (0-100)
- PDF Report Export
- Offline Mode (Critical)

### OUT OF SCOPE (Phase 2+)
- VIN OCR / Barcode Scanning
- Marketplace Listings Integration
- In-app Payment Processing
- Mechanic Marketplace
- Social Sharing Features
